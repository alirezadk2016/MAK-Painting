About MAK Painting Group

Craftsmanship
You Can See

MAK Painting Group was founded on a simple belief: that every surface deserves the best possible finish. Based in Melbourne, we bring together master painters with a passion for perfection and a commitment to using only the finest materials.

From luxury residences in Brighton to large-scale commercial fit-outs in the CBD, our portfolio speaks to a consistent standard of excellence that has made us one of Melbourne's most trusted painting companies.

  • Fully licensed & insured in Victoria
  • Premium Dulux & Taubmans certified applicators
  • 5-year workmanship guarantee on all projects
  • Clean, respectful and punctual teams
